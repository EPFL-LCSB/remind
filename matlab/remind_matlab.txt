MATLAB version for ReMIND package

This module requires matTFA:
https://github.com/EPFL-LCSB/matTFA
Tested with solvers IBM CPLEX (v12.7.1)

Run:
1) the file_setup.m to set up the files, solver and prepare the models
2) DiMEs_main.m to generate alternative uptake and secretion profiles
3) make_ILP.m to constract and solve the ILP to identify interactions
